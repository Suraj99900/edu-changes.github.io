    EDUCATION

    Everyone inside and outside of K-16 education blames “the system.” We redesigned it.

    This isn’t your daily lesson plan. This is architecture for lifelong learning. It’s time for everyone to learn how to learn…and to trust themselves as learners.

    The Integrated Science Program

    We pioneered Open Learning Architecture™ to reinvent science class for ages 13-20. Global issues & PBL power 36+ modules & competency-based assessment. 4 implementation models deliver coherent, rigorous learning for real-world STEM.

    Concept Construxions®

    This patented system utilizes color & shape recognition to support healthy discourse, co-construction of knowledge, and critical thinking. Designed for coherence, it deepens conceptual understanding across disciplines & over time.

    Professional Development

    Immersive professional development for both of our learning systems is a cornerstone of our work. Job-embedded, ongoing PD supports successful implementation. The approach is seamless for teachers & leaders serious about change.
